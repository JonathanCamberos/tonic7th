# Intervals

Intervals are the distance between two notes. They are the foundation of melody, harmony, and ear training.

## Core ideas

- A **minor second** is one half step.
- A **major second** is two half steps.
- An **octave** is eight notes apart and contains twelve half steps.

## Why intervals matter

Intervals define the color of chords and melodies.
In this lesson, the sheet music example helps you see how intervals move from one pitch to the next.

### Practice

Listen while you follow the score. Try naming the interval between each pair of notes.
