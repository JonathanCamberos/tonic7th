# Major Scales

Major scales are built from a repeating pattern of whole and half steps.

## Pattern

The major scale pattern is:

- whole
- whole
- half
- whole
- whole
- whole
- half

## What to listen for

In the example score, follow the stepwise motion and notice how the scale returns to the tonic note.

### Study tip

Count the half steps between notes and compare them to the major scale pattern.
