# Triads

Triads are three-note chords built from stacked thirds. They are the simplest and most common chords in tonal harmony.

## Types of triads

- **Major triad**: root, major third, perfect fifth.
- **Minor triad**: root, minor third, perfect fifth.
- **Diminished triad**: root, minor third, diminished fifth.
